🧠 Idea lab · hooks & campaigns

📋 Content ideas

  • ◆ "Day in the life" of a small biz owner (Reel)
  • ◆ Carousel: 3 myths about [industry] debunked
  • ◆ "This or That" story polls for product feedback
  • ◆ Behind‑the‑scenes of packaging orders
  • ◆ UGC roundup: real customers using the product

🎯 Campaign ideas

  • ◆ #MyMorningRoutine (branded hashtag challenge)
  • ◆ "5 days, 5 tips" educational series
  • ◆ Limited drop teaser with countdown stickers
  • ◆ Partner with micro‑influencer for honest review
  • ◆ "Comment your fave emoji for a DM discount"

✍️ Caption examples

  • ◆ "POV: you finally found a skincare routine that doesn't break the bank. 🫶"
  • ◆ "we heard you like iced coffee … so we made it even smoother. ☕️"
  • ◆ "Send this to the friend who needs a self‑care Sunday."

🪝 Hooks that work

  • ◆ "Stop scrolling if you want better engagement."
  • ◆ "I tried posting at 6am for a week — here's what happened."
  • ◆ "The one metric most small businesses ignore."
  • ◆ "Why your boosted post isn't converting (and how to fix it)."
